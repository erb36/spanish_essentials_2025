Know-how:

'Hello' or 'goodbye' depend on the time of day. In Spanish the day is divided into four parts: la manana (morning), la tarde (afternoon), la noche (evening -night).

'Buenos dias' is the right phrase to use until lunch time (normally between 12.00 and 14.00).

Use 'Buenas Tardes' until 17.00/18.00 and 'buenas noches' s from dinner time (normally between 19.00 and 21.30) until you go to bed. Use 'buenas noches, hasta mañana' only to say goodbye to someone before going home after dinner or to wish someone a good night before they go to bed.
